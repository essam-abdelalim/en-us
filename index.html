Who I am

Senior Presenter at Al Jazeera Network

I am an Algerian senior presenter who works for Al Jazeera channel in Qatar. I was born in the city of Lakhdaria in 1977 AD. I also graduated from the Institute of Economic Sciences at the University of Algiers.I learned to speak Arabic, English, French, and some Italian.

I started my career as a journalist for some Algerian newspapers, such as Al-Shorouk Al-Arabi and Al-Asil. I covered the International Monetary Fund and World Bank in Dubai in 2003. I then worked for CNBC Arabia in Dubai as a stock watcher and presenter for 8-9 years. In 2013, I joined Al Jazeera channel in Qatar as a producer and broadcaster. Now, I am a senior presenter at Al Jazeera and a very grateful man for the life I am living now.
